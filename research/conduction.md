Conduction Theory
=====================
The equation for conduction from Tielens is simply:

$$ K(T) = K_0 T^{5/2}$$

$$ \frac{dT}{dt} = \frac{0.6 m_H}{1.5 k_B} \frac{d}{dx} (K(T) \frac{dT}{dx})$$
